# Contoso HR — Company Policy Document

## Dress Code Policy

### General Guidelines
Contoso maintains a business-casual dress code for all employees. The goal is to present a professional appearance while allowing comfort and individual expression.

### Office Days (Monday–Thursday)
- **Acceptable:** Collared shirts, blouses, slacks, chinos, closed-toe shoes, blazers
- **Not Acceptable:** Athletic wear, flip-flops, tank tops, ripped jeans, graphic t-shirts
- **Client Meeting Days:** Business formal required (suit/blazer with dress pants or equivalent)

### Casual Friday
- Jeans and sneakers are permitted
- Company-branded t-shirts are encouraged
- All other guidelines still apply (no beachwear, gym clothes, etc.)

### Remote Work
- When on video calls, business-casual from the waist up is expected
- No dress code enforcement for audio-only calls

## Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

### Annual Allocation
| Tenure | PTO Days | Sick Days | Personal Days |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0–2 years | 15 | 10 | 3 |
| 3–5 years | 20 | 10 | 5 |
| 6+ years | 25 | 10 | 5 |

### Requesting PTO
1. Submit requests through the HR portal at least **2 weeks** in advance
2. Manager approval required for requests of 3+ consecutive days
3. Blackout periods: Last 2 weeks of December, first week of Q4 close

### Carryover
- Up to 5 unused PTO days can roll over to the next year
- Sick days do not roll over
- Personal days do not roll over

## Remote Work Policy

### Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day probation period are eligible for hybrid remote work.

### Schedule
- **Default:** 3 days in-office, 2 days remote per week
- **In-office days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday (core collaboration days)
- **Remote days:** Monday and Friday (flexible)

### Equipment
- Contoso provides a laptop, monitor, keyboard, and mouse for home office
- Employees must have reliable internet (minimum 25 Mbps download)
- Reimbursement of up to $50/month for internet costs upon receipt submission

### Communication
- Must be available on Microsoft Teams during core hours (9 AM – 5 PM local time)
- Camera on for team meetings
- Respond to messages within 1 hour during core hours
