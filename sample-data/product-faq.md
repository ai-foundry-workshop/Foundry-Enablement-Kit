# Contoso HR — Product & Benefits FAQ

## Health Insurance

**Q: What health insurance plans does Contoso offer?**
A: Contoso offers three plans through Aetna:
- **Basic Plan:** $150/month employee contribution, $2,000 deductible, 80/20 coinsurance
- **Standard Plan:** $250/month, $1,000 deductible, 90/10 coinsurance
- **Premium Plan:** $400/month, $500 deductible, 100% coverage after deductible

**Q: When is the benefits enrollment period?**
A: Open enrollment runs **November 1–15** each year. New hires can enroll within 30 days of their start date.

**Q: Can I add dependents to my plan?**
A: Yes. Spouse, domestic partner, and children under 26 are eligible. Add dependents during open enrollment or within 30 days of a qualifying life event (marriage, birth, adoption).

## 401(k) Retirement Plan

**Q: Does Contoso offer a 401(k) match?**
A: Yes. Contoso matches **100% of the first 4%** and **50% of the next 2%** of your salary contribution. Maximum company match is 5% of your annual salary.

**Q: When can I start contributing?**
A: Immediately upon hire. The company match begins after 90 days of employment.

**Q: What is the vesting schedule?**
A: Company match vests over 3 years:
- Year 1: 33%
- Year 2: 66%
- Year 3: 100%

## Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

**Q: What services does the EAP provide?**
A: Free confidential counseling for:
- Mental health (anxiety, depression, stress)
- Financial planning and debt management
- Legal consultation (up to 30 minutes)
- Work-life balance coaching

**Q: How do I access EAP services?**
A: Call the EAP hotline at 1-800-555-0199 (24/7) or visit eap.contoso.com. Services are free for all employees and immediate family members.

## Learning & Development

**Q: Does Contoso reimburse tuition?**
A: Yes. Up to **$5,250/year** for job-related courses at accredited institutions. Must maintain a B average or higher. Submit reimbursement through the HR portal with proof of enrollment and grades.

**Q: What about certifications?**
A: Contoso covers the full cost of pre-approved professional certifications (e.g., Azure, AWS, PMP, CISSP). Submit a request to your manager before registering. One certification attempt per year is covered; retakes are at the employee's expense.

**Q: Are there internal training programs?**
A: Yes:
- **Contoso Academy:** Monthly workshops on leadership, communication, and technical skills
- **Mentorship Program:** Pair with a senior leader for 6 months (apply in January and July)
- **LinkedIn Learning:** Full access for all employees via SSO

## Commuting & Parking

**Q: Does Contoso offer commuter benefits?**
A: Yes. Pre-tax commuter benefits up to $300/month for public transit and $300/month for parking through WageWorks.

**Q: Is parking available at the office?**
A: Limited parking is available on a first-come, first-served basis. Monthly garage passes are $150/month (pre-tax). Apply through Facilities on the HR portal.
