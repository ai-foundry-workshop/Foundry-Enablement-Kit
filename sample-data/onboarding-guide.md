# Contoso HR — New Employee Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to Contoso!

This guide walks you through your first 30 days. Complete each section in order. Check boxes as you go.

---

## Day 1: Getting Started

### Morning (9:00 AM – 12:00 PM)

- [ ] **Arrive at the front desk** — Security will issue your badge and direct you to your team area
- [ ] **Meet your manager** — 30-minute welcome meeting, review of your first-week plan
- [ ] **Pick up your equipment:**
  - Laptop (Dell or MacBook, depending on your team)
  - Monitor, keyboard, mouse
  - Headset for Teams calls
  - Welcome kit (notebook, pen, company swag)

### Afternoon (1:00 PM – 5:00 PM)

- [ ] **IT setup session (1:00–2:30 PM):**
  1. Power on your laptop
  2. Sign in with your @contoso.com credentials (sent to your personal email)
  3. Complete multi-factor authentication (MFA) setup using Microsoft Authenticator
  4. Install required software: Microsoft 365, Teams, Outlook, VPN client
  5. Connect to Wi-Fi network: `Contoso-Corp` (password provided by IT)
  6. Test email: send a test message to your manager
  7. Test Teams: join the `#new-hires` channel and post an introduction

- [ ] **HR orientation (3:00–4:30 PM):**
  - Benefits overview presentation
  - Sign employment documents
  - Set up direct deposit
  - Choose health insurance plan (within 30 days)
  - Review and sign the employee handbook acknowledgment

## Week 1: Learning the Ropes

### Day 2
- [ ] Team introduction meeting (your manager will schedule)
- [ ] Review team documentation on SharePoint
- [ ] Set up your development environment (if engineering role)
- [ ] Complete mandatory compliance training on Contoso Academy (3 modules, ~2 hours)

### Day 3
- [ ] Shadow a team member on their daily work
- [ ] 1-on-1 with your manager: discuss 30-60-90 day goals
- [ ] Complete security awareness training (1 module, ~45 minutes)

### Day 4
- [ ] Begin your first assigned task (your manager will assign)
- [ ] Attend your first team standup/daily meeting
- [ ] Explore the HR portal: bookmark key pages (PTO, expenses, IT support)

### Day 5
- [ ] Friday team social (optional but encouraged)
- [ ] End-of-week check-in with manager
- [ ] Submit any remaining HR documents

---

## Week 2–4: Ramping Up

### Week 2
- [ ] Complete remaining compliance training modules
- [ ] Attend department all-hands meeting
- [ ] Set up your personal development goals in the HR portal
- [ ] Request access to any additional tools/systems needed for your role

### Week 3
- [ ] Start contributing to team projects
- [ ] Schedule coffee chats with 3 people outside your immediate team
- [ ] Review your benefits selections (make changes before 30-day deadline)

### Week 4
- [ ] 30-day check-in with manager
  - Review progress on 30-day goals
  - Discuss any challenges or needs
  - Set priorities for days 31–60
- [ ] Complete the new hire survey (link sent via email)
- [ ] Finalize all HR enrollment (health, 401k, commuter benefits)
